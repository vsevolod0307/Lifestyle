Bring me the horizon
British rock band from Sheffield, Yorkshire, formed in 2004. It currently consists of vocalist Oliver Sykes, guitarist Lee Malia, bassist Matt Keane, drummer Matt Nicholls, and keyboardist Jordan Fish.
Imagine dragons
American indie rock band formed in 2008 in Las Vegas, USA. They became known after the release of their debut studio album Night Visions in September 2012. American magazine Billboard named them the brightest new stars of 2013 and the biggest band of 2017, and Rolling Stone magazine named their single "Radioactive" the biggest rock hit of the year..
Three days grace
Canadian alternative metal and post-grunge rock band. Was formed under the name Groundswell in Norwood, Ontario, Canada in 1992. In 1997, the band, consisting of guitarist and vocalist Adam Gontier, drummer Neil Sanderson and bassist Brad Wolst, changed its name to the current one.
